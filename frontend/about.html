Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Parkin.

Is Parkin available in all malls in Egypt? ›
We are currently active in major malls in Sheikh Zayed, New Cairo, 6th of October, Heliopolis, and Alexandria. We are expanding rapidly to cover all major commercial hubs.
How do I pay for my parking session? ›
You can pay by credit/debit card, mobile wallet, or Parkin Wallet. The payment page allows real input data for cardholder, card number, expiry date, and CVV.
Can I reserve a spot days in advance? ›
Reservation policy depends on the destination. Most malls allow same-day reservations, while event destinations support advance bookings.
